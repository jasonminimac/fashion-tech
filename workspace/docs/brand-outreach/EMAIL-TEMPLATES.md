## Email Best Practices

- **Subject line:** Specific, benefit-driven, curiosity (not spammy)
- **Length:** 3–4 short paragraphs max
- **Tone:** Professional but friendly, no jargon
- **CTA:** Clear and specific ("20-min call next week?" not "let's discuss")
- **Personalization:** Name, role, recent company news (shows research)
- **Follow-up:** 7 days after initial send; 14 days after follow-up
